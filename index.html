Macro and Adjustment Rules

Protein: 180 g/dayCarbs: 220 to 250 g/dayFat: 65 to 75 g/day

  • If body weight does not drop 0.5 to 1.0 lb/week for 2 weeks, reduce carbs by 25 g/day.
  • If strength drops hard for more than 1 week, add 20 to 30 g carbs around workout.
  • One refeed day every 10 to 14 days: increase carbs by 60 to 80 g, keep fat lower.
  • Fiber target: 30 to 40 g/day from vegetables, fruit, legumes, oats, seeds.

Supplements (discuss with your doctor)

  • Creatine monohydrate 3 to 5 g/day
  • Vitamin D3 (dose by blood test)
  • B12 for vegetarian diet
  • Algae omega-3 (EPA+DHA)
  • Magnesium glycinate before sleep if needed

Weekly Conditioning and Recovery

  • Zone 2 cardio twice weekly: 30 to 40 minutes brisk incline walk or cycling.
  • One short interval day: 8 rounds of 30 sec hard, 90 sec easy.
  • Yoga session once weekly: 25 to 35 minutes.
  • Sleep target: 7.5 to 8.5 hours.
  • Desk reset each hour: 2 to 3 minutes walk + chest opener.
Shoulder rehab progression: if pain is above 4/10, reduce load, use neutral-grip pressing and extra cuff work.

Medical Checks to Request

  • CBC, ferritin, iron panel
  • Vitamin D, B12, zinc
  • TSH, free T3, free T4
  • HbA1c and fasting insulin if needed

If crown and mid-scalp continue to thin after 4 to 6 months despite routine and corrected labs, dermatology review is strongly recommended.

No natural method matches finasteride/minoxidil efficacy in most studies, so set realistic expectations and monitor objectively.
